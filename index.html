Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find the hidden Konig parts?

The Konig parts are scattered across the map. The bumper is often found near the junk piles at the scrapyard, while other parts are hidden in the forest. We are compiling a full map with coordinates soon.

How do I mix fuel for the chainsaw?

You need a Jerry Can with gasoline and a bottle of Two-Stroke Oil. Pour the oil into the Jerry Can to create the mixture.

What does the 'Friendship' stat do?

Building friendship with NPCs unlocks specific perks. For example, higher friendship with the Parts Store owner may give you discounts or access to rare parts.
