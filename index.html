Common Questions

Where is the Konig front bumper?

It's located at the Scrapyard (Jim's U-Pull), lying on the ground near the back fence. It's free!

What is the wrench size for wheels?

All wheel lug nuts use a 13mm wrench.

How do I unlock the Race Track?

You need to raise your friendship with Leo. Give him Poutine daily until he gives you the key.
