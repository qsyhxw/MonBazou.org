Mon Bazou Map Locations & Secrets

Mon Bazou doesn't have a GPS. Getting lost in the Canadian woods is easy. Use this guide to find key locations like the Junkyard, the Weed Barn, and hidden car parts.

Key Locations (Legend)

  • 1. Jim's U-Pull (Scrapyard) Location: Past the main town, follow signs on the highway.
    Why go here: Buy cheap parts, find the Konig Bumper, sell scrap metal. Open 08:00-17:00.
  • 2. The Race Track Location: Near the main garage intersection. Look for the big gate.
    Why go here: Test top speed. Requires Key from Leo.
  • 3. Calito's Barn (Weed Dealer) Location: Hidden in the forest. Look for Blue Lights visible at night.
    Why go here: Buy seeds, sell packs ($$$).
  • 4. The Sugar Shack Location: West side forest.
    Why go here: Your main base for making Maple Syrup.

Hidden Free Items

Don't buy these parts. Find them for free.

  • Konig Front Bumper: Lying on the ground inside the Scrapyard (Check near the back fences/piles).
  • Konig Side Skirts: Found at the Cottage (near the lake).
  • Spoiler: Random spawn at Scrapyard.
