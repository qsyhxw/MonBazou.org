Mon Bazou Money Guide: From Wood to Syrup

Running low on cash for your Konig build? In Mon Bazou, money doesn't grow on trees—but it does flow out of them. Whether you're just starting with a rusty axe or managing a massive sugar shack operation, this guide covers the most efficient ways to earn cash.

1. Early Game: The Grind (Active Income)

When you first start, you need cash for gas, food, and basic tools. Do not try to start a syrup empire yet; the setup costs are too high. Focus on these quick cash grabs.

Woodcutting (Selling Logs)

The most reliable way to make your first $1,000. You don't need to split the wood yet; just load raw logs into your truck/ATV and drive to the Sawmill.

  • Location: The Sawmill (Check map for green circle drop-off).
  • Pine Trees (Best Value): Sell for $15 per log.
  • Maple Trees: Sell for $10 per log. (Warning: Save these for syrup tapping later!)
  • Birch Trees: Sell for $8 per log.
Pro Tip: Focus on cutting Pine trees (the tall, skinny ones) early on. They sell for 50% more than Maple trees.

Pizza Delivery

Once your vehicle runs, you can work for the pizza shop in town. It opens around 5-6 PM.

  • Payout: Approximately $50 - $100 per shift (Base pay + tips).
  • Note: Tips depend on speed. Don't expect GTA-level payouts here; this is just for gas money.

2. Mid Game: Stability

Selling Firewood to Francis

Once you have a Wood Splitter, stop driving to the Sawmill. Sell split firewood to your brother, Francis, who lives near your starting house.

  • Efficiency: 1 Log = 2 Firewood pieces.
  • Limit: Francis buys about 60 pieces per day.
  • Strategy: Use this to get rid of low-value Birch wood or scrap wood near your base.

Scrapyard (Jim's U-Pull)

Drag old car parts (rusty doors, bumpers) into the dumpster outside the Scrapyard gate. Payout is based on weight. Heavy parts = more cash.

3. Late Game: The Maple Syrup Empire

Syrup is the king of Mon Bazou. It provides a unique "weekly paycheck" system that stabilizes your economy.

The Barrel Economy

You don't get paid all at once. The Federation pays you a pension.

Payment Type Amount Timing
Upfront Payment $200 Immediate (Drop-off)
Weekly Pension $200 / week Every Sunday (for 5 weeks)
Total per Barrel $1,200 Over 5 weeks
Why it's OP: If you sell 10 barrels this week, you receive a $2,000 paycheck every Sunday for the next month, even if you do nothing.

4. Cannabis Farming (High Risk)

Once you unlock the Barn (check Phone Book for dealers), you can sell in bulk.

  • Goal: Create a "Huge Cannabis Pack" (2,700 joints).
  • Payout: $10,000 flat per pack.
  • Requirement: Requires the Bunker, lights, and planters. It's a huge investment but offers the biggest single lump-sum payment in the game.
