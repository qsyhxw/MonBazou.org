How to Get Gifts

  • Poutine: Buy from the Snack Shack trailer in town (~$10) or cook at home.
  • Maple Syrup: Use your own supply (boil sap) or buy a can at the Sugar Shack.
  • Beer: Buy a pack at the Grocery Store. Good backup gift.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock the Race Track?

You must raise your friendship with Leo to approximately 50-75. Once the level is high enough, talk to him at the garage/track entrance, and he will give you the key.

How do I give a gift to Mom?

You cannot give physical items to Mom. You must use your phone (at home or mobile) and dial 555-2109 once a day. Eventually, she will mail you a package containing the Fryer Basket.
