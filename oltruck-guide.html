OlTruck Repair & Upgrade Guide

The Konig is for racing, but the OlTruck is for making money. You need this pickup truck to haul massive loads of wood and maple syrup barrels. It spawns at your house, but it's in terrible condition.

Important: Do not try to drive the truck immediately. The brakes are seized and it will not stop!
ADVERTISEMENT

Required Parts List (Restoration)

Go to the Parts Store or Junkyard to find these replacements. The stock parts are usually at 0% condition.

  • 4x Brake Calipers (Most important!)
  • 1x Starter Motor
  • 1x Alternator
  • 1x Car Battery
  • 4x Spark Plugs
  • 1x Oil Filter

Tip: Check the Bolt Sizes Page for tools needed to remove these parts.

Why fix the OlTruck?

  • Hauling Capacity: It can fit 6+ Maple Syrup barrels or a huge pile of logs.
  • Towing: It has more torque for towing other vehicles or trailers.
  • Off-Road: With upgrades, it handles the forest dirt roads better than the Konig.
ADVERTISEMENT

Recommended Upgrades

1. Suspension Lift Kit

Available at the Parts Store. This raises the truck, allowing you to drive over logs and rocks in the forest without getting stuck.

2. Bed Cap / Rails

Helps keep the wood logs from falling out of the back when you hit a bump.
